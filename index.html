About Finchball

Welcome to the world of Finchball! As the head coach of the University of Hawaii football team, I lead a style of play that embodies hardnosed, aggressive, spread-option football. Finchball is not just another strategy; it's the most effective and innovative style of play that college football has ever seen. Our approach blends traditional grit with cutting-edge tactics, allowing us to outmaneuver and outplay our opponents. We pride ourselves on being at the forefront of football innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the field.

Team Philosophy

Our team believes in playing with heart and determination. Finchball is not just about winning; it's about pushing limits, taking risks, and giving everything for the love of the game. We thrive on challenges and strive to create a dynamic and electrifying experience for our fans. Our commitment to excellence, discipline, and teamwork sets us apart and drives us to achieve greatness both on and off the field.

Join the Team

Are you ready to take your game to the next level? At the University of Hawaii, we offer a unique opportunity for aspiring athletes to grow and succeed. Our program is not only about developing elite football skills but also about building character, leadership, and resilience. As a Rainbow Warrior, you'll train in a stunning paradise, with state-of-the-art facilities and a supportive community that feels like family.

We are committed to providing our players with the best coaching, resources, and opportunities to excel. Whether you're looking to make an immediate impact or develop over time, we have a place for you. Our coaching staff, led by experienced professionals, will guide you to reach your full potential, both athletically and academically.

If you're passionate about football and ready to embrace the Finchball philosophy, the University of Hawaii is the perfect place for you. Join us, and become part of a team that is rewriting the history of college football.
